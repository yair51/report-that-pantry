How To Get Your Pantry Status Setup

1. Register your organization on the "Add Organization" page. If your organization is already registered, skip to step 2.

2. Create an account on the "Sign Up" page

3. Add your pantry location on the "Add Locations" page

4. Print out the poster and place it on the front of your pantry (see example below)

5. Manage your notification preferences on the "Manage Notifications" page
