Our Mission

Report That Pantry is a student-run organization dedicated to connecting communities and fighting food insecurity through Little Free Pantries. We empower pantry stewards with real-time information and tools, while making it easier for volunteers and those in need to find and support these vital community resources.

What is a Little Free Pantry?

Little Free Pantries are small, often homemade boxes where people can leave non-perishable food items and hygiene products for their neighbors in need. They operate on the honor system, with anyone free to take what they need or leave what they can. Report That Pantry makes it easy for pantry stewards and users to keep track of the contents in these pantries.
