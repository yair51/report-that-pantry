How Report That Pantry Works

Connecting communities to keep Little Free Pantries stocked and accessible

Our Mission

Report That Pantry is a community-driven platform that helps Little Free Pantries increase their visibility and maintain consistent stock levels through real-time monitoring and community engagement.

Increase Visibility

Make pantries discoverable to those who need them

Monitor Stock Levels

Track pantry fullness in real-time

Community Engagement

Connect volunteers and donors

How It Works

1

Pantry Registration

Pantry owners and volunteers register their Little Free Pantry locations on our platform. This includes adding photos, descriptions, and precise location coordinates to make pantries easily discoverable.

2

Real-Time Monitoring

Community members report pantry stock levels by taking photos and estimating fullness percentages. Our AI technology helps analyze images to provide accurate stock assessments.

3

Community Alerts

When pantries run low on supplies, our system automatically notifies registered volunteers and community members who can help restock them.

4

Easy Discovery

Anyone in need can easily find nearby pantries using our interactive map, complete with real-time stock levels and directions to each location.

5

Continuous Support

Our platform provides ongoing analytics and insights to help communities understand usage patterns and optimize pantry operations for maximum impact.

Benefits for Communities

For Pantry Beneficiaries

Easy access to food assistance with real-time information about stock availability and locations.

For Pantry Volunteers

Efficient monitoring and restocking alerts to ensure pantries remain well-stocked and accessible.

For Community Organizations

Data-driven insights to understand community needs and optimize resource allocation.

For Donors

Transparency about where donations are needed most and the impact of their contributions.
